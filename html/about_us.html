<♠> Card Fellows is the brainchild of Nicholas Carignan. Our goal is two-fold; we wanted to build a fun website to play Blackjack on, and also teach the basics of Blackjack and the science behind odds based play. With these ingredients we hope that players will have fun playing while similtaneously reinforcing the knowledge that gambling is a dangerous way to try and make money, and should be done for fun and not for proffit.

Nicholas Carignan and Andrew Bloom worked long hours crafting the game code, using Nicholas' knowledge of cards to create a solid virtual version of the game. Jeff Martinez put his experience and skills into the meta-game working on stats tracking and data manipulation functions. Dustin Mundy spent his time crafting art assets for the various pages and building the card elements out of CSS and HTML. </♣>
