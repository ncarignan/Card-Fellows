Basic Blackjack Rules:

  • In a game of blackjack, both the dealer and the player are dealt two cards. One card is face up, one is face down.
  • The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.
  • If the total value of the cards in your hand goes over 21, this is called a bust and you lose the bet
  • Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 11 or 1, the ace changes to a 1 if your hand would bust.
  • To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn.
  • If you go over 21 and bust, you lose your bet regardless of the dealer's hand.
  • If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & any card worth 10), you have a blackjack and win unless the dealer also has blackjack.
  • The dealer will hit until their cards total 17 or higher.
